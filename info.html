what is a Ghazal?

Ghazals are poetry, music, and longing woven into words. Their meanings shift like light on water—fluid, elusive, never quite the same twice. Below, you’ll find handpicked ghazals, paired with translations that aim not to replace, but to reveal the essence hidden between the lines.

why translate the untranslatable?

The translations of ghazals online often lose their soul. This project is an attempt to give them back some of their magic. Instead of word-for-word translation, I focus on evoking the feeling, rhythm, and intent behind each verse.

why should you care?

Because poetry is meant to be felt, not just understood. If you've ever heard a ghazal and felt something stir in your chest, this is for you. Here, translation isn't a substitute—it’s a bridge, carrying you closer to the emotions hidden in every verse.
